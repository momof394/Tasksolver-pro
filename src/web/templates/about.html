How We Support Ourselves

You might wonder how we keep this platform free. Here's our transparent approach:

  • Voluntary Support: Users who find value in our platform can choose to support us through voluntary donations.
  • Partnerships: We may partner with wellness-focused organizations that align with our values.
  • Ethical Advertising: If we ever include ads, they'll be relevant, non-intrusive, and focused on wellness and personal development.
  • Consulting: We offer consulting services for organizations wanting to implement similar wellness-focused tools.

What we'll never do: Sell your data, create artificial limitations, or charge for basic features.

Our Story

Tasksolver Pro began as a task management platform but evolved into something much more meaningful. We realized that what people really needed wasn't just another productivity tool, but a holistic platform that supports their overall well-being while helping them create and share valuable content.

The transformation happened when we started focusing on the human element - the stress, the need for self-care, the desire to help others, and the barriers that prevent people from accessing tools for personal growth. That's when we decided to make everything free and focus on wellness.
